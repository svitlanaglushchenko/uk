About me

Greetings, my friends!!!

My name is Svitlana Hlushchenko. I am a certified photographer from Irpin, Ukraine.

I specialize in art photography, and I also love photographing interesting people, emphasizing their individuality.

I received my education at the Kyiv photography school. I cooperate with the most famous online resource for selling works of photo art YOURDAILYPHOTOGRAPH (USA). Participant of the online project «PhotographersForUkraine».
