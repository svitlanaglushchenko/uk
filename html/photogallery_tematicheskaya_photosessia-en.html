Thematic photosession
reveal yourself in a new way

A themed photo session in the style of Harry Potter will allow your child to immerse himself in the atmosphere of a fairy tale, have a good time, get unforgettable impressions, great photos in digital form and in the format of a photo book.

Exclusive entourage props are provided for the photo shoot.

The shooting time is 2.5 hours. Filming is carried out in the studio and in the open locations of the park, Naberezhnaya of Irpin river, etc.

In addition to electronic media, your photos after digital processing will be turned into a photo book that will not get lost in gigabytes of other information, and will delight you every time you want to take it off the shelf.
