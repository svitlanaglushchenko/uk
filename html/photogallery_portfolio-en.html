Actor's portfolio
a pass to the world of art

Actor's portfolio is necessary for both adults and children - everyone who dreams of trying himself as an actor or a participant in a TV show. Having a portfolio with a full palette of portraits of your emotional range will allow you to stand out favorably already at the preliminary stage of casting.

Clothes - in no case over-size to adequately assess the physique. Give preference to neutral, calm colors and not too active prints. Clothes should be comfortable!
For boys: jeans/trousers (not tracksuits!) + long-sleeved shirt/hoodie/t-shirt.
For girls: jeans + sweatshirt/long-sleeved T-shirt, plain dress (sheath, straight, school sundress), basic skirt, incl. school + calm top.

Hair - as you usually wear, the main thing is clean and carefully dried. Do not make complex hairstyles, curls, super braids.

Makeup - categorically not! Let's assume a light foundation, mascara. A must for EVERYONE - moisturizing lips with hygienic lipstick, neat manicure.

Must have: change of shoes with clean soles (preferably with closed toes), a comb.

P.S. The most important thing is a good mood. Positive setting during photography - 80% of success!!!
