ARTPHOTOGRAPHY
The space of my creativity and self-realization

The series of photographs "Dan.9:27" was made in Western Europe. This series was launched in France in January 2022. The second birth took place already in Germany, during the evacuation from the territory of Ukraine, temporarily occupied by the ruSSist invasion.

My own psychological state, thoughts about Ukraine and the future were realized through the retouching of their own photographs of local architecture. My thoughts were with my native Irpin, and I blurred the contours of the French streets beyond recognition, thought about wounded Kharkiv, destroyed Mariupol and took layer by layer from the color panoramas of Paris.

On the one hand, I understood that today my children are safe in the temporary shelter behind the codon. I don't have to explain to them the principle of two walls during enemy shelling of a residential area. At the same time, I was perfectly aware that evil, not recognized in time, fueled by compromises and corrupted by impunity, can reach any European city, no matter how far west it is, and turn it into ashes. The hand pressing the button of the infernal machine will not be stopped by the beauty of historical or modern works of art and architecture.

The phantom ghosts of modern cities should remind EVERYONE at what cost our fighters keep the assets of the civilized world from chaos.

«PhotographersForUkraine»

Project «PhotographersForUkraine», ORGANIZER - YOURDAILYPHOTOGRAPH (USA) 2022 , it is made on a platform @OpenSea in NFT format. Out of more than 1000 received images, only 40 were selected by the curators of the project, including my work. I am glad that I was able to join a common cause, which also leads to victory over the RuSSists. All funds from the sold works go directly to the charity fund UkraineDao, an organization that provides humanitarian aid to Ukrainians: supplies food, medicine, etc. Share this link on all social networks available to you so that as many collectors as possible can add a cool NFT to their collection at the same time and help our country in the fight for every human life.
