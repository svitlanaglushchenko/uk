Babyborn
the first photosession of your baby

Babyborn photosession is a photo session of babies aged 4-10 days.

It is in these first days after birth that babies feel comfortable in those positions that are traditionally used for photography and are very similar to those that the baby recently took in the mother's womb. Usually, during photography, babies doze, or even sleep soundly, without feeling any discomfort.

You can sign up for a photo session in the last months of pregnancy. It is desirable to do this even before the birth of the baby, because the time frame for filming is significantly limited to the first two weeks after birth. If you book in advance, you can be sure that even with a heavy workload of the photographer, your baby will definitely get great photos of the first days of his life. There is no need to make a previous payment.

Photography takes place at your home with the use of additional props (entourage birch crib, glasses, clothes, toys, etc.). All items are constantly treated with hypoallergenic washing and treatment with an ultraviolet lamp. It is possible to use the services of a specialized studio in Kyiv.

If you have any questions, I will be happy to answer them by phone or via social media.
