Greetings, my friends!!!

My name is Svitlana Hlushchenko. I am a certified photographer from Irpin, Ukraine.

I specialize in art photography, and I also love photographing interesting people, emphasizing their individuality.

I received my education at the Kyiv photography school. I cooperate with the most famous online resource for selling works of photo art YOURDAILYPHOTOGRAPH (USA). Participant of the online project «PhotographersForUkraine». My works have been repeatedly exhibited at photo art exhibitions in Kyiv.

I value life. I'm moving forward. I believe in victory!!!
